Our Heritage

A School of Leaders

Ntare School was founded in 1956 by the Congregation of the Holy Ghost Fathers in Mbarara, western Uganda — established to provide quality education while developing strong moral values and leadership skills among young men.

Over the decades, Ntare has grown into one of Uganda's premier secondary schools, revered for its academic rigour, discipline, and unique capacity to forge capable leaders. It has earned its reputation as the "School of Leaders" across East Africa.

Among its most distinguished alumni are Heads of State, shaping the destiny of entire nations.
